Our Project

Labelling & Boxing Automation System

Project Overview

Problem Statement

The current pharmaceutical packaging line at ATH Sdn Bhd faces a critical operational gap between the existing feeding and capping stages and the final distribution phase, as there is currently no automated system for labelling and boxing. This lack of integration results in production inefficiencies and bottlenecks that prevent the modular system from reaching its full output potential. Furthermore, the absence of real-time data acquisition capabilities hinders the ability to perform remote monitoring and predictive diagnostics, increasing the risk of unexpected equipment breakdowns. Operational safety is also a concern, as the line requires a standardized emergency stop system and high-visibility status indicators specifically a tri-color tower light to comply with ISO 13850:2015 standards and ensure personnel safety during impending danger.

Solution

The proposed solution is the development of an automated Labelling and Boxing Station that integrates seamlessly with the existing tablet feeding and capping systems at ATH Sdn Bhd. The system is designed using PLC-based control architecture to ensure precise coordination of conveyor movement, labelling application, and boxing operations within a continuous production flow.


To enhance operational efficiency and reduce downtime, the system incorporates real-time monitoring using Node-RED, enabling remote supervision, data acquisition, and predictive maintenance capabilities. This allows operators to detect abnormalities early and improve overall system reliability.


In addition, safety is reinforced through the implementation of an ISO 13850:2015-compliant emergency stop system and a tri-color tower light indicator (Red, Yellow, Green) to provide clear visual status feedback during operation. The overall design ensures a modular, scalable, and industry-ready automation system suitable for pharmaceutical production environments.

Project Objectives

  • To design and develop an automated Labelling and Boxing Station integrated with the existing tablet feeding and capping production line.
  • To implement PLC-based control for accurate coordination of conveyor movement, labelling, and boxing processes.
  • To enable real-time monitoring and data acquisition using Node-RED for improved system visibility and remote diagnostics.
  • To ensure system safety by implementing an ISO 13850:2015-compliant emergency stop system and tri-color tower light indicators.
  • To develop complete engineering documentation including PLC ladder diagrams, pneumatic schematics, and mechanical layouts for system implementation.
  • To establish a structured 10-year asset management plan to support long-term system maintenance and sustainability.

System Features

  • PLC-based automation system for coordinated control of labelling, boxing, and conveyor operations.
  • Integrated Labelling and Boxing Station designed to work seamlessly with existing tablet feeding and capping systems.
  • Real-time monitoring and data acquisition using Node-RED dashboard for system performance tracking and diagnostics.
  • Industrial safety system including ISO 13850:2015-compliant emergency stop and tri-color tower light (Red, Yellow, Green).
  • Pneumatic actuator system for precise mechanical movement and packaging operations.
  • Modular system design allowing scalability, maintenance, and future expansion of production line.
